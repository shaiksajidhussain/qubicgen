About RPA

What is Robotic Process Automation? (RPA aka BPA=Business Process Automation)

RPA is a technology that automates standardized, repeatable processes. It does three things well: it reduces cost, improves quality, and improves operational controls.

What is Business Process Automation CoE (Center of Excellence)?

An RPA COE is a cross-functional team with the clear objective of deploying RPA automations at scale as quickly, as efficiently, and as safely as possible.
